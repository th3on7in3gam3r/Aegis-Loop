Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Aegis Loop uses cookies and similar technologies on our website and dashboard.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. They help websites remember your preferences and keep you signed in.

Cookies we use

  • Session cookie (aegis_sid) — Essential. Keeps you signed in to the dashboard after GitHub OAuth or PAT authentication. Contains only a random session identifier — no tokens or personal data. HTTP-only, signed, expires after 7 days.
  • Theme preference (aegis-theme) — Functional. Stores light or dark mode in localStorage on your device (not sent to our servers).
  • Cookie consent (aegis-cookie-consent) — Essential. Records whether you accepted optional cookies. Stored in localStorage.

Essential vs optional

Essential cookies are required for authentication and core functionality. They cannot be disabled while using the signed-in dashboard.

Optional cookies — we currently use none. There is no analytics or tracking script on this site; storage is limited to authentication and preference cookies.

Managing cookies

You can clear cookies through your browser settings. Signing out of Aegis Loop removes your session cookie. Clearing site data will reset theme and consent preferences.

Third-party cookies

GitHub OAuth may set cookies when you authenticate on github.com. We do not control third-party cookie policies.

Updates

We may update this Policy when our cookie practices change. Check the date above for the latest version.
